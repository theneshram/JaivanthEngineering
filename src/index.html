Welcome to Jaivanth Engineering

Jaivanth Engineering is a professionally managed manufacturing company specializing in industrial components and cold-forged products, headquartered in Coimbatore, India. Established in 2000, we have built a strong reputation for precision, reliability, and consistent quality, serving customers across diverse industrial sectors.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification, modern infrastructure, and a skilled workforce, we deliver products that meet stringent quality standards and customer expectations.

About Jaivanth Engineering

Our Story & Strengths

Company Profile

Jaivanth Engineering is one of the established manufacturers of machined, pressed, and cold-forged industrial components. Founded by Mr. R. Subramaniam, the company operates from its own manufacturing facility in Ganapathy, Coimbatore, covering approximately 10,000 sq. ft.

Over the years, Jaivanth Engineering has continuously upgraded its machinery, processes, and quality systems, enabling it to meet evolving customer and regulatory requirements with confidence.
